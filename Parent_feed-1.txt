We are highly satisfied with the overall quality of education provided at Vishwakarma Institute of Information Technology. The institution maintains strong academic standards, experienced faculty members, and a well-structured curriculum that helps students build both theoretical knowledge and practical skills. The teaching methodology, continuous assessment process, and focus on industry-oriented learning have contributed significantly to our confidence in the institution’s ability to prepare students for successful careers. Compared to many other institutions, VIIT has created a balanced academic environment that encourages learning, innovation, and overall personality development.


The placement performance at Vishwakarma Institute of Information Technology has been extremely impressive and remains one of the institution’s strongest aspects. The consistent placement records, reputed recruiting companies, and attractive salary packages provide parents with confidence regarding their child’s future career prospects. The efforts taken by the placement cell in conducting training sessions, mock interviews, aptitude preparation, and industry interactions are clearly visible in the placement outcomes. This strong placement support gives parents assurance that students are receiving the right exposure and opportunities needed to succeed professionally.


While the college administration generally shares important information regularly, communication regarding academic events, examination schedules, holiday notices, and other updates could still be more timely and systematic. At times, parents receive information at short notice, making it difficult to plan accordingly. Introducing a more structured communication system through mobile notifications, emails, and regularly updated portals would help parents stay informed and connected with college activities more effectively.


The hostel facilities provided by the institution are satisfactory overall, with proper security arrangements, cleanliness, and accommodation support for students. However, the quality, variety, and consistency of the food served in the hostel mess require considerable improvement. Students often expect healthier meal options, better hygiene standards, and greater variety in the menu. Since many students stay away from home for long durations, improving food quality would greatly enhance student comfort, health, and overall hostel satisfaction.


The mentorship program introduced by the college is highly appreciated by parents because it helps maintain regular monitoring of students’ academic performance, attendance, and personal development. Mentors act as an important bridge between students, faculty, and parents, ensuring that concerns are addressed at an early stage. This initiative gives parents confidence that students are receiving proper guidance and support throughout their academic journey, which contributes positively to discipline, motivation, and academic growth.


The fee payment system is organized and transparent, but introducing more flexible installment options would greatly help many parents manage financial responsibilities more comfortably. Education expenses can sometimes place financial pressure on families, especially when multiple payments are scheduled within short durations. Providing additional installment schemes, extended deadlines, or customized payment plans would make the process more convenient and parent-friendly while reducing financial stress.


The college website and parent portal contain useful information, but the overall user experience could be improved significantly. Parents would appreciate a more user-friendly interface that allows easier access to attendance records, academic performance, fee status, examination details, and important announcements. Simplifying navigation and improving portal responsiveness would strengthen communication between the institution and parents while making monitoring of student progress more convenient.


One of the most reassuring aspects of Vishwakarma Institute of Information Technology is the strong focus on campus safety and security. The presence of security personnel, CCTV surveillance, controlled campus access, and disciplined campus management creates a safe and secure environment for students. As parents, this provides peace of mind and confidence that the institution prioritizes student well-being alongside academic excellence.


We would strongly appreciate more frequent parent-teacher interaction meetings during the academic year, as these sessions help parents stay informed about their child’s academic progress, behavior, strengths, and areas needing improvement. Increased interaction between parents and faculty would strengthen collaboration in supporting students and create a more transparent and supportive educational environment. Regular meetings, whether conducted online or offline, would further enhance trust and communication between the institution and families.
