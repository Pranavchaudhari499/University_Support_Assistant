OVERALL SENTIMENT

The overall student sentiment toward Vishwakarma Institute of Information Technology is mixed but largely positive. Students appreciate the strong placement support, approachable faculty members, quality library resources, and vibrant campus activities. However, several operational and infrastructure-related concerns continue to affect the overall student experience. Key issues include poor hostel WiFi connectivity, declining canteen food quality, delayed communication regarding examinations, outdated laboratory equipment, and technical issues with the fee payment and attendance portals. While students generally enjoy campus life and academic opportunities, improvements in infrastructure and administrative efficiency are strongly expected.

TOP ISSUES
Poor WiFi connectivity in hostel areas, particularly during late evening hours, making it difficult for students to attend online lectures, complete assignments, and prepare for exams.
Declining canteen food quality along with reduced menu variety, leading to dissatisfaction among students who depend heavily on campus food services.
Delay in syllabus completion by certain faculty members, creating academic pressure and stress for students during examinations.
Outdated laboratory equipment in some departments, limiting hands-on learning experiences and reducing exposure to modern technologies.
Delayed updates in the attendance management system and frequent crashes in the fee payment portal, especially near deadlines, causing confusion and frustration among students.
Late communication of examination schedules and academic notifications, affecting student preparation and planning.
POSITIVE HIGHLIGHTS
The placement cell has received strong appreciation from students for its excellent training, placement preparation activities, and successful recruitment outcomes with reputed companies.
The library facilities and digital academic resources, including research platforms such as IEEE Xplore, are highly valued for supporting academic learning and research activities.
Faculty members are generally approachable, supportive, and helpful during doubt-solving sessions, creating a positive academic environment.
Sports infrastructure and annual sports events contribute positively to campus life and student engagement.
Lab sessions are organized systematically, helping students gain practical exposure despite infrastructure limitations in some laboratories.
KEY RECOMMENDATIONS
Upgrade hostel internet infrastructure to ensure stable and high-speed WiFi connectivity throughout the day, especially during peak study hours at night.
Improve canteen food quality, hygiene standards, and menu variety to provide healthier and more satisfying meal options for students.
Ensure timely completion of syllabus across all subjects through better academic monitoring and scheduling practices.
Modernize laboratory infrastructure by replacing outdated equipment and introducing industry-relevant tools and technologies.
Improve the reliability and performance of the attendance and fee payment portals to prevent technical issues during important periods.
Communicate examination schedules, academic events, and important notices earlier to help students plan their studies more effectively.
Continue strengthening placement training initiatives and maintaining strong industry connections to sustain high placement performance.
FINAL SUMMARY

Students generally view Vishwakarma Institute of Information Technology as an academically supportive institution with strong placement opportunities, good faculty interaction, and an engaging campus environment. However, infrastructure-related concerns, administrative delays, and technical issues in student services require immediate attention to further improve the overall student experience and institutional satisfaction.
